# Blinkit_Sales_Analysis_Project

Project Overview

This project involves a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit’s sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to identify key business insights
. Using a dataset of approximately 8,500 grocery orders, an interactive and dynamic Power BI dashboard was developed to track performance across various metrics such as outlet location, item types, and fat content
.

Business Requirements

The primary objective was to define and track Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and detailed charts to understand the business's health
:

1. KPIs: Total Sales (overall revenue), Average Sales (revenue per sale), Number of Items (total count of products sold), and Average Rating (customer satisfaction)
.

2. Specific Analysis:
- Total Sales by Fat Content: Analysing the impact of fat content on sales using Donut charts
.
- Total Sales by Item Type: Identifying performance across various categories like dairy, soft drinks, and household items
.
- Outlet Establishment Analysis: Evaluating how the age and establishment year of an outlet affect its performance
.
- Sales by Outlet Size and Location: Comparing revenue generated by different outlet sizes (High, Medium, Small) and locations (Tier 1, 2, and 3)
.

Technical Workflow

1. Data Connection: Connected Power BI to a raw Excel dataset containing 12 fields and over 8,000 rows
.

2. Data Cleaning: Used Power Query Editor to resolve data discrepancies, such as standardizing labels (e.g., converting "LF" and "low fat" to "Low Fat") and checking for null values or errors
.

3. DAX Implementation: Developed custom measures using DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) to calculate total revenue, average ratings, and a dynamic rolling count of items
.

4. Dashboard Design: Created a visually appealing layout utilizing Blinkit’s brand colours (yellow and green) and implemented interactive features like URL filters and reset buttons
.
5. Interactivity: Configured edit interactions to ensure filters and slicers (like outlet size and item type) dynamically update the entire dashboard rather than just highlighting data
.

Key Insights

- Revenue Performance: The analysis revealed $1.2 million in total sales with an average sale value of $141
.
- Consumer Preference: Products with low fat content were found to be more popular, representing approximately 65% of total orders
.
- Top Categories: Fruits, vegetables, and snack foods emerged as the top-selling item types by volume
.
- Outlet Success: Tier 3 locations and medium-sized outlets proved to be the highest-performing segments in terms of total revenue
.
- Growth Trends: Outlets established in 2018 showed significantly higher performance compared to those started in other years
.

Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop: For data visualization and dashboard creation
.
- Power Query: For ETL processes and data cleaning
.
- DAX: For advanced calculations and business logic
.
- Excel: As the primary data source
.
